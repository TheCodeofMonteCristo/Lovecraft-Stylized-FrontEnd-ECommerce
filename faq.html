Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the Lovecraftian E-commerce site?
It is a mock e-commerce platform where cities are sold as commodities under the control of the Lovecraftian squid aliens.
How can I purchase a city?
Simply browse the available cities, choose your preferred one, and proceed to the "Buy City" page to confirm your purchase.
Are there any guarantees with my city purchase?
No. Once a city is bought, there are no refunds. As per the Lovecraftian rules, you may be left to your own devices in the alien-ruled landscape.
How do I leave a review?
You can leave a review for a city on its individual page. We welcome any feedback on your experience with the cities!
Who do I contact if I have a problem?
Visit our "Contact" page to reach out to the alien customer service representatives.
